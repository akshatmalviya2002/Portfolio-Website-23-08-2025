Project Overview

This graduation project focused on developing Open Guard, a KYC software by IdendefAi, designed to simplify KYC through data collaboration across institutions. The goal was to create an MVP with a stellar visual design to attract investors. While initially limited to designing screens and components, the project expanded with in-depth UX research to refine features and establish a complete brand identity. Open Guard aimed to enhance KYC efficiency, reduce costs, and leverage a network-based approach for seamless data sharing.
