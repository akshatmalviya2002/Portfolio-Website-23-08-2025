About

Akshat Malviya

Currently working at Accenture as a UI/UX Designer. I love crafting creative solutions for difficult problems. Interfaces for me are a blend of logic, layout and aesthetics. I see designing as a way of putting down the craziness from my mind in the form of creativity and then sculpting it down with logic and thinking to form a solution. I am an active software enthusiast and love to try out new softwares just for fun!

Other than designing, I love cooking, sketching, cycling, PC games, and of course simping over Batman.
