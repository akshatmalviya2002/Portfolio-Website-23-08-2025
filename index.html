About Me

Akshat Malviya

Currently working at Accenture as a UI/UX Designer.

I love crafting Creative solutions for difficult problems. Interfaces for me are a blend of Logic, Layout and Aesthetics. I see designing as a way of putting down the craziness from my mind in the form of Creativity and then sculpting it down with Logic and Thinking to form a solution. I am an active Software enthusiast and love to try out new softwares just for fun!

Other than Designing, I love Cooking, Sketching, Cycling, PC games, and of course simping over Batman.
